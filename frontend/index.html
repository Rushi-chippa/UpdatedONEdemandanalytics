How Demandalytics Works

1 Upload Your Dataset

Drag & drop or browse your local file system to load data files (supported formats: .csv, .xlsx, .xls).

2 Automatic Synthesis & Diagnostics

Our analytics engine instantly parses your columns to identify key categorical segments, date intervals, and numeric metrics. It computes MoM growth, anomaly indicators, and trains customizable predictive machine learning models.

3 AI Consultations & Exporting

Leverage the conversational strategic assistant, filter diagnostic views interactively, and export state-of-the-art PDF/PPTX executive summaries dynamically themed to match your chosen dashboard branding.

Recommended Column Headers:
  • Category / String: Store, Dept, Region, SKU, etc. (Categorical)
  • Dates: Date, Timestamp (formatted as DD-MM-YYYY or YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Metrics / Numeric: Weekly_Sales, Sales, Revenue, Profit, etc.
